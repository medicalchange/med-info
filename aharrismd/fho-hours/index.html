Daily rounding

Track actual minutes by category, then bill in cumulative 15-minute units per day. Remainders of 8 minutes or more round up.

Core limits

14 payable hours per day. 240 hours per rolling 28 days. The OMA calculator also caps indirect + clinical admin at 25% of billable time and clinical admin at 5%.
